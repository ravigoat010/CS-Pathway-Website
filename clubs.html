    Robotics

    The MHHS Robotics Club is a club for students interested in the STEM field to come and learn about robotics. Students participate in the VEX competitions and learn core skills such as teamwork, leadership, and much more. Students are encouraged to apply the Engineering Design process and professionally create a robot to do a specific task, and are required to apply basic engineering principles such as the Engineering Notebook in the process. This club is for anyone, regardless of experience. This club is a great way to bring brilliant minds together and is a great example of how Engineering and Computer Science can be applied in real life.

    Hackathon

    The MHHS Hackathon Club increases student learning through workshops, provides the opportunity to earn community service hours, and aids in career discovery in computer science fields. Students are also encouraged to apply their learning at Hackathon competitions, where they can compete and earn valuable prizes. The Hackathon Club immerses students further into computer science and related fields and provides them with complementary knowledge to the content they are being taught in their high school classes.

    Esports

    It is a space for fans of video games both casual and competitive to come together and have fun! The goal is to create a closely knit community that can bond together through a love for video games. The games members can particpate in include League of Legends, Super Smash Bros, Valorant, and etc. The ESports club fosters a community where people can come to have fun playing games and connect socially with their peers.

    Skills USA

    SkillsUSA empowers its members to become world-class workers, leaders and responsible American citizens. We improve the quality of our nation’s future skilled workforce through the development of SkillsUSA Framework skills that include personal, workplace and technical skills grounded in academics. Our vision is to produce the most highly skilled workforce in the world, providing every member the opportunity for career success.
